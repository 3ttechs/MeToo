Welcome to METOO APP

The METOO APP is a meeting scheduler app and this tutorial demonstrates (helps) use of this APP.
STEP 1 : SIGN UP / LOGIN
STEP 1A: Sign Up - Create your login with your mobile/cell phone number and also by providing your name and email ID
STEP 1B: Login with Social network IDs - Login with Social network IDs option (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn).
STEP 1C: Login - Login with your mobile/cell phone number if you have Signed up already.

Next step in using METOO APP?

STEP 2: ADD CONTACTS

Start adding contacts. Fill the name, phone number and other details especially email ID.

Next step in using METOO APP?

STEP 3: ADD MEETING

Start scheduling meetings. Select a date, Fill the subject, location and other details especially Start and End timings.

Next step in using METOO APP?

STEP 4: MY SCHEDULE

Looking at My Schedule, Past and Future meetings.Select Future meetings to view and to Accept/ Decline by putting suitable comments.

Next step in using METOO APP?

STEP 5: FEEDBACK

Providing feedback for individual meetings.Choose any in the 5 star rating. Fill your comments if any in the text box.
